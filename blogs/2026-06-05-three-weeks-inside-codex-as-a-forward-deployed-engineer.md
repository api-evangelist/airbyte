Dallas Inman, a Forward Deployed Engineer at Airbyte, spent three weeks using Codex and found that the product experience around an AI agent can matter as much as the model itself. Codex's work-first design—requiring no mode selection and featuring built-in browser functionality—better suited his daily workflow of customer interaction, bug testing, documentation, and coding compared to mode-based alternatives.
